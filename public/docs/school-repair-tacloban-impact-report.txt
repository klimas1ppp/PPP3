PROJECT IMPACT REPORT

Project:        Classroom Rebuild — Brooke's Point, Palawan
Project ID:     PPP-2026-0221-SCHOOL
Report date:    February 21, 2026
Category:       Education

------------------------------------------------------------
1. SUMMARY
------------------------------------------------------------
After typhoon damage left an elementary classroom unusable,
donated yield funded a new roof, structural repairs, 40 desks,
and a full set of learning materials in Brooke's Point,
southern Palawan. 140 students returned to safe, dry learning
conditions.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. OUTCOMES
------------------------------------------------------------
- 140 students back in a safe classroom.
- Construction overseen by the local parent-teacher association.
- Every invoice published on-chain for full transparency.
- Maintenance reserve set aside for ongoing upkeep.
