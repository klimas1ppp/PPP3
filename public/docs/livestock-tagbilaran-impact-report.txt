PPP — PRINCIPAL-PRESERVING PHILANTHROPY
PROJECT IMPACT REPORT

Project:        Goats & Poultry Micro-Farms — Roxas, Palawan
Project ID:     PPP-2026-0402-LIVESTOCK
Report date:    April 2, 2026
Category:       Farming & Livestock

------------------------------------------------------------
1. SUMMARY
------------------------------------------------------------
A livestock starter program provided breeding goats, laying
hens, feed, and veterinary care to 18 families (approx. 96
individuals) in rural Roxas, central Palawan. Each household
received husbandry training and a record-keeping system.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. OUTCOMES (first 8 weeks)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Families selling surplus eggs at the local market.
- First independent income generated from the project.
- "Pay-it-forward" herd model multiplies impact: the first
  female offspring from each goat is passed to a new family.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. FUNDING
------------------------------------------------------------
Total yield deployed:        6,200 USDC
  Breeding goats (24)          3,000 USDC
  Laying hens & coops          1,500 USDC
  Feed & veterinary care       1,100 USDC
  Husbandry training             600 USDC

All disbursements are recorded on-chain (Base) and verifiable.
